# Azores Logistics and Budget Notes

The Azores reward travelers who keep the route simple. Inter-island flights and
ferries can consume time, so a short or moderate trip is usually better with
one main island and perhaps one nearby add-on. Sao Miguel alone can fill a
week with Sete Cidades, Furnas, Lagoa do Fogo, coastal viewpoints, and thermal
pools.

Costs are moderate if apartments and local restaurants are used, but rental
cars can sell out in summer. Without a car, tours can replace driving but raise
daily spend.

The practical advice is to book accommodation and car rental early, keep
weather-flexible hike days, and choose the Azores when the user values scenery,
quiet, and nature over guaranteed beach heat.
