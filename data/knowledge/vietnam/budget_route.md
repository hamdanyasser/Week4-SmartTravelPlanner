# Vietnam Budget Route Notes

Vietnam is one of the best long-trip value destinations in this set. A traveler
can keep daily costs low with guesthouses, street food, trains, buses, and
locally booked tours. That makes it attractive for two weeks when the budget is
fixed.

For a hiking-focused route, the plan should not stretch from north to south.
Hanoi, Ninh Binh, Pu Luong, and Ha Giang or Sapa is already enough. Ha Giang is
excellent for scenery but is more road-trip than pure hiking unless planned
with local walks.

Vietnam is a strong answer when budget and culture matter heavily. It is less
clean for a user who wants predictable July weather and easy mountain hiking.
