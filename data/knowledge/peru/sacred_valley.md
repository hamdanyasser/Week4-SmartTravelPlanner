# Peru Sacred Valley and Andes Briefing

Peru is a powerful culture-and-adventure destination anchored by Cusco, the
Sacred Valley, Machu Picchu, Lake Titicaca, Arequipa, and Andean trekking.
For hikers, the Inca Trail, Salkantay, Lares, and day hikes around the valley
provide strong options.

July is dry season in the Andes, which is excellent for trekking visibility.
It is also high season, so permits, trains, and guided treks need early
planning. Nights at altitude can be cold even when days are sunny.

Best fit signals: hiking, archaeology, culture, mountain scenery, and a
once-in-a-lifetime feel. Reality pressure: altitude, advance permits, and
flight distance.
