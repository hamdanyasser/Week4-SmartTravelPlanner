# Canary Islands Base Selection Notes

The Canary Islands can be either a package-resort trip or an active volcanic
island trip. For AtlasBrief's hiking-focused user, the recommendation should
steer toward towns with trail access, local food, and quieter evenings rather
than the densest resort strips.

Tenerife works well with a split between the north and a mountain-access base.
Gran Canaria works well when the traveler explores the interior rather than
staying only on the coast. Public transport exists, but cars simplify hiking.

This destination is a safe fallback for warm weather and accessible hiking,
especially when Madeira flights are expensive or the user wants more beach
time.
