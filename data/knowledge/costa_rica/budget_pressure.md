# Costa Rica Budget Pressure Notes

Costa Rica can be expensive compared with nearby Central American destinations.
Park fees, guided tours, transfers, and popular eco-lodges add up quickly. For
a two-week itinerary, the budget pressure depends heavily on flight origin and
how many regions the traveler tries to combine.

Travelers who want to stay near a modest budget should limit the route to two
or three bases, use shared shuttles, and choose locally owned lodges. The
country can still fit a careful plan, but it is not the safest first pick for a
tight budget if flights are already high.

This makes Costa Rica a useful counterfactual: it often wins the dream side,
but reality pressure can rise when airfare, tours, and July weather are
included.
