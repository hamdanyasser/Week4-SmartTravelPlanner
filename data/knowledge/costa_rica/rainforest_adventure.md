# Costa Rica Rainforest Adventure Briefing

Costa Rica is an obvious adventure answer because it combines rainforest,
volcanoes, wildlife, beaches, rafting, zip lines, and cloud-forest hiking in a
compact country. Arenal, Monteverde, Manuel Antonio, and the Osa Peninsula all
offer strong nature experiences with established traveler infrastructure.

For a user asking for warm weather and hiking, the dream fit is high. The
tradeoff is cost and season. July falls in the green season: landscapes are
lush and mornings can be bright, but afternoon rain is common. Popular parks
also draw international travelers, so "not too touristy" requires choosing
smaller lodges or less famous regions.

Best fit signals: wildlife, rainforest, volcano hikes, warm climate, guided
nature activities, and a classic adventure travel story.
