# Albania Alps and Coast Overview

Albania offers a strong budget-adventure mix: the Accursed Mountains in the
north, Ottoman towns such as Gjirokaster and Berat, and Ionian beaches along
the Riviera. The Theth to Valbona hike is the country's signature mountain
route, with guesthouses and dramatic limestone scenery.

The fit is high for travelers who want a less obvious European summer trip.
The tradeoff is logistics. Roads, buses, and transfers can be slower than in
Slovenia or Portugal, and July beach areas can be busy and hot. The mountains
are the better match for hiking and lower tourist pressure.

Best fit signals: lower cost, mountain hiking, warm weather, cultural towns,
and a feeling of discovery compared with mainstream Mediterranean routes.
