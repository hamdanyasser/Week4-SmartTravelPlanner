# Albania Budget and Logistics Notes

Albania is one of the stronger European choices for travelers watching cost.
Guesthouses, local meals, and intercity transport are usually cheaper than in
Western Europe. That makes it attractive for a two-week trip with a fixed
budget.

The main pressure is planning complexity. Mountain routes may require minivans,
ferries, guesthouse coordination, and cash. Beach towns can become crowded in
July, especially around Sarande and Ksamil, so the best itinerary should not
depend only on the coast.

A practical plan pairs northern mountain hiking with Berat or Gjirokaster and
one quieter beach stop. Albania is best recommended when the user is flexible,
curious, and comfortable with rougher logistics in exchange for value.
