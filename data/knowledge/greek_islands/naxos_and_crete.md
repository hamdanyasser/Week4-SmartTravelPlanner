# Naxos and Crete Active Islands

The Greek Islands are not only beaches. Naxos and Crete can support a more
active trip with village walks, mountain drives, ancient sites, food, and
coastal hikes. Crete has gorges and larger-scale landscapes, while Naxos feels
more compact and relaxed than the most famous Cycladic islands.

July is reliably warm and sunny, but it is also peak season. The destination
fits warmth and culture easily; it fits "not too touristy" only if the route
avoids Santorini, Mykonos, and the busiest cruise windows.

Best fit signals: sun, islands, food, ancient sites, village walks, and easy
summer logistics. Reality pressure: crowds and higher accommodation prices.
